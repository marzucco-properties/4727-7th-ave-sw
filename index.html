Property Overview

A Rare Estate Opportunity

4727 7th Avenue SW is a custom-built single-story CBS ranch situated on a fully uplands 2.50-acre estate parcel in the Collier Woods section of Golden Gate Estates. Built in 1999 by G. Gifford Construction, the home features 5 bedrooms, 3.5 bathrooms, and approximately 3,547 square feet of conditioned living area with a 3-car garage and screen-enclosed pool and spa. A brand-new 2025 TAMKO Heritage shingle roof, verified 2021 Rheem 5-ton HVAC condenser, 100% uplands certification, Estates zoning permitting horses, livestock, and a future guest house, and no active HOA combine to place this property in an increasingly rare category as developable acreage in Golden Gate Estates continues to be absorbed.
